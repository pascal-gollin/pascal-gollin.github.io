about me

I am a Postdoc at FAMNIT at the University of Primorska, Slovenia. I work in graph theory with a focus on structural graph theory of both finite and infinite graphs. I obtained my PhD in mathematics in 2019 from the University of Hamburg, under the supervision of Reinhard Diestel. From 2019 to 2024, I was a research fellow in the Discrete Mathematics Group (DIMAG), which is part of the Pioneer Research Center for Mathematical and Computational Sciences within the Institute for Basic Science (IBS) in South Korea.
